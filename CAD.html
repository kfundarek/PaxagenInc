CAD: Computer Aided Design

CAD models are fundamental to modern design and manufacturing processes. They are essential for almost any idea of a product to becoming a reality. Manufacturers use them to simulate the movement of a part through the production process. For instance, it allows them to simulate feed rates, speeds of tooling, the position of the clamps that hold the part, and many other possibly limiting factors of the machines that they will use to fabricate the part. Often times CAD files are required to program the machines that will be used in a part’s fabrication. Please contact us if you are looking to have CAD models developed.
